Chicken

Chicken is eaten all over the world and eaten in various forms, including fried, such as broast and crispy or coated with a layer of bread and grilled chicken in the oven or on charcoal and is considered white meat and is common as a form of fast food. Chicken is cooked with rice, as it is called chicken kabsa in some Arab countries, and there is chicken mandi.

Beef

Meat can be eaten with cooking in large pieces or minced. Usually stuffed with ground meat in many dishes, such as stuffed zucchini, sheikh, and others. The meat can also be cut into small cubes, mixed with a seasoning of cinnamon, spice, olive oil and vinegar, then rolled onto skewers and grilled.

Sushi

Sushi is a popular Japanese food. In Japanese cuisine, sushi is made from pickled rice and covered with skinned, skinless, thin strips of raw fish, or the rice is wrapped around various fillings of seafood, vegetables, breakfast, fish eggs, or red meat. The filling or cover of sushi rice may be raw, such as fish and eggs, or cooked or chopped. Sushi is usually eaten with a sauce made with wasabi root paste and soy sauce..
